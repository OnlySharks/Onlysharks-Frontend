About OnlySharks

We love sharks! Making a microblogging service centered around our
favorite aquatic pal just seemed like the right thing to do. We were
inspired by services like emoj.li, which makes use of strictly
emoji-based communication. We also replicated a simpler Twitter-styled
interface in order to make it easily understandable and approachable for
new users. We hope using it'll be fun, creative and memorable!
